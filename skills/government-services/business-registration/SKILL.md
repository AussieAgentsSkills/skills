# Australian Business Registration

You are an expert in registering businesses in Australia.

## Business Structures

| Structure | Best For | Liability | Tax |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-----|
| Sole Trader | Simple, low risk | Personal | Individual rates |
| Partnership | 2+ people, shared | Partners liable | Partner rates |
| Company (Pty Ltd) | Growth, protection | Limited | 25-30% flat |
| Trust | Asset protection | Trustee liable | Beneficiary rates |

## ABN Registration

### Who Needs ABN
- Carrying on enterprise in Australia
- GST registration required
- Dealing with government

### How to Apply
1. Go to abr.gov.au
2. Complete online application
3. Provide TFN
4. Describe business activity
5. Receive ABN instantly (most cases)

**Free** - no cost for ABN

### ABN Requirements
- Must be genuine business
- Not hobby or one-off sale
- Must have TFN

## Business Name Registration

### When Required
- Trading under name different from your own
- Not required if sole trader using own name

### How to Register
1. ASIC Connect: asic.gov.au
2. Search availability
3. Register online
4. Pay fee

### Fees (2024)
| Period | Cost |
|--------|------|
| 1 year | $42 |
| 3 years | $99 |

### Rules
- Must not be identical/similar to existing
- Cannot include restricted words (bank, trust, etc.)
- Must renew before expiry

## Company Registration (Pty Ltd)

### Requirements
- At least 1 director (18+, Australian resident)
- At least 1 shareholder
- Registered office address (Australian)
- Company name (or ACN as name)

### How to Register
1. ASIC Connect or registered agent
2. Choose company name
3. Provide director/shareholder details
4. Pay registration fee: $576

### Ongoing Obligations
- Annual review: $299 (small) / $1,408+ (large)
- Keep registered office
- Maintain records
- Lodge changes within 28 days

## GST Registration

### Must Register If
- Turnover ≥$75,000 (or $150,000 non-profit)
- Taxi/rideshare driver
- Want to claim GST credits

### How to Register
- Online via Business Portal
- Part of ABN application
- Effective date can be backdated

### What You Must Do
- Charge GST on taxable sales
- Issue tax invoices
- Lodge BAS (monthly/quarterly/annually)
- Keep records 5 years

## PAYG Withholding

### Must Register If
- Employing staff
- Withholding from payments

### How to Register
- Business Portal
- Part of ABN application

### Obligations
- Withhold from wages per tax tables
- Report on BAS
- Lodge via STP
- Provide payment summaries

## TFN for Business

### Sole Trader
Use your personal TFN

### Company/Trust
Apply for separate TFN via ATO

## Super (Employer)

### Must Pay If
- Employee earns ≥$450/month (removed 2022)
- All employees now entitled

### Choose Default Fund
- Compare via YourSuper: ato.gov.au/yoursuper
- Must be MySuper product
