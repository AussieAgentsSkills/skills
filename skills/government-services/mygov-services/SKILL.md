# myGov Services Guide

You are an expert in Australian government services accessible through myGov.

## myGov Linked Services

### Medicare
- **Medicare card:** Covers GP visits, hospital, some specialists
- **PBS:** Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (subsidised medications)
- **Safety Net:** Extra subsidies after spending threshold
- **Bulk billing:** Doctor charges Medicare directly (free for patient)

### Centrelink
- **JobSeeker:** Unemployment payments
- **Youth Allowance:** Students and young job seekers
- **Parenting Payment:** Single or partnered parents
- **Age Pension:** Retirement income support
- **Disability Support Pension:** Permanent disability
- **Carer Payment/Allowance:** For those caring for others
- **Family Tax Benefit:** Help with child costs

### ATO via myGov
- Lodge tax returns
- View super accounts
- Payment plans
- Income statements (replaced payment summaries)

### Child Support
- Register
- Make/receive payments
- Update details

### My Health Record
- View health records
- Control access settings

## Key Thresholds (2024-25)

### Medicare Safety Net
| Threshold | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Original (concessional) | $560.40 |
| Extended | $2,544.30 |
| Greatest Permissible Gap | $99.70 |

### Centrelink Income Test (JobSeeker)
- Income free area: $150/fortnight
- Taper rate: 50 cents per dollar over threshold
- Partner income test applies

### Age Pension
- Single: ~$1,116.30/fortnight
- Couple combined: ~$1,682.80/fortnight
- Assets test and income test apply

## Common Tasks

### Lost Medicare Card
1. Log into myGov
2. Go to Medicare
3. Request replacement card
4. Or call 132 011

### Report Centrelink Income
- Report every 2 weeks via:
  - myGov app
  - Centrelink online
  - Phone reporting

### Link Service to myGov
1. Sign into myGov
2. Select "Link another service"
3. Verify identity (linking code or questions)
