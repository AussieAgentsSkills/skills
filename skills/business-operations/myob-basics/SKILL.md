# MYOB Basics for Australia

You are an expert in using MYOB accounting software for Australian businesses.

## MYOB Products

| Product | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| MYOB Business Lite | Sole traders, simple needs |
| MYOB Business Pro | Small business, employees |
| MYOB AccountRight | Complex business, inventory |
| MYOB Advanced | Mid-market, multi-entity |

## Australian Setup

### GST Configuration
- Settings > Tax Codes
- Default codes: GST (10%), FRE (GST-free), N-T (No Tax), INP (Input taxed)
- Set reporting: Cash or Accrual basis

### BAS Reporting
- Reports > Tax > BAS
- Review GST collected (1A) and paid (1B)
- Check PAYG withholding (W1-W4)
- Lodge via ATO portal

### Single Touch Payroll (STP)
- Enabled by default in MYOB
- Reports automatically each pay run
- Finalise by 14 July

## Chart of Accounts

Standard Australian structure:
- 1-xxxx: Assets
- 2-xxxx: Liabilities
- 3-xxxx: Equity
- 4-xxxx: Revenue
- 5-xxxx: Cost of Sales
- 6-xxxx: Expenses

## Bank Feeds

Supported banks:
- All major Australian banks
- Credit unions
- Building societies

Setup: Banking > Bank Feeds > Add Account

## Payroll Setup

### Employee Records
- TFN
- Super fund details
- Tax declarations
- Pay rates and categories

### Pay Items
- Wages (ordinary, overtime)
- Allowances
- Deductions
- Super (SGC + additional)
- Leave accruals

### Pay Run Workflow
1. Create pay run
2. Review hours/amounts
3. Process pay
4. STP automatically reported
5. Pay super (quarterly minimum)

## BAS Workflow

1. **Reconcile bank accounts** 
2. **Review transactions** - correct GST codes
3. **Run BAS report** - Reports > Tax
4. **Review figures** - compare to prior periods
5. **Export/Lodge** - via accountant or ATO

## Invoicing

Tax invoice requirements auto-included:
- ABN (from company setup)
- GST amounts
- "Tax Invoice" label
- Sequential numbering

## Super Payments

Via MYOB:
- Payroll > Pay Superannuation
- Connects to clearing house
- Tracks due amounts
- Reminder for quarterly deadline

## MYOB vs Xero

| Feature | MYOB | Xero |
|---------|------|------|
| Desktop option | Yes (AccountRight) | No |
| Inventory | Strong | Basic |
| Payroll | Included | Extra cost |
| Learning curve | Steeper | Easier |
| Bank feeds | Good | Excellent |

## Common Issues

1. **GST code wrong** - Review before BAS
2. **Duplicate entries** - Check bank feeds vs manual
3. **Super not calculating** - Check employee setup
4. **STP errors** - Verify TFN and details
5. **Bank rec not matching** - Check opening balance
