# Wages & Pay Rates

Everything about what you must pay employees.

## Core Concepts

### [[wages/minimum-wage|National Minimum Wage]]
The absolute floor — $24.10/hr as of July 2025. No one can be paid less (except juniors/apprentices with specific rates).

### [[wages/casual-loading|Casual Loading]]
Casuals get 25% loading instead of leave entitlements. Makes casual minimum $30.13/hr.

### [[wages/penalty-rates|Penalty Rates]]
Extra pay for weekends, public holidays, overtime, late nights. Rates vary by award — see [[MOCs/awards]].

### [[wages/overtime|Overtime]]
Usually time-and-a-half for first 2-3 hours, then double time. Check your award for specifics.

### [[wages/allowances|Allowances]]
Extra payments for tools, travel, uniforms, meals, first aid duties, etc.

### [[wages/superannuation|Superannuation]]
12% on top of wages, paid to employee's super fund. Quarterly deadline.

## Quick Rates (July 2025)

| Type | Hourly Rate |
|------|-------------|
| Adult minimum | $24.10 |
| Casual minimum | $30.13 (incl. 25% loading) |
| Sunday (retail) | ~$48.20 (200%) |
| Public holiday | ~$60.25 (250%) |

## Pay Frequency

- Must be at least monthly
- Most common: weekly or fortnightly
- Pay slips required within 1 day of payment

## Underpayment

Underpaying employees is illegal. Penalties:
- Back-pay owed wages
- Interest
- Fines up to $93,900 per breach (individuals) or $469,500 (companies)

If you've underpaid, fix it immediately. FWO may reduce penalties for voluntary disclosure.
