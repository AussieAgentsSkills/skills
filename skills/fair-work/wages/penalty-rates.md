# Penalty Rates

Higher rates for working outside normal hours. Specific rates depend on your [[MOCs/awards|award]].

## Common Penalty Rates

### Retail (General Retail Industry Award)

| When | Full-time/Part-time | Casual |
|------|---------------------|--------|
| Saturday | 125% | 150% |
| Sunday | 200% | 200% |
| Public Holiday | 250% | 275% |

### Hospitality (Hospitality Award)

| When | Full-time/Part-time | Casual |
|------|---------------------|--------|
| Saturday | 125% | 150% |
| Sunday | 150% | 175% |
| Public Holiday | 250% | 275% |

### Note on Casual Rates

Casual penalty rates are calculated on the **base rate**, not the casual-loaded rate.

```
Casual Sunday (Retail):
Base rate: $24.10
Sunday rate: $24.10 × 200% = $48.20

NOT: $30.13 × 200%
```

The 25% casual loading is already factored into the penalty percentage.

## Overtime

When employees work beyond ordinary hours:

| Overtime Period | Rate |
|-----------------|------|
| First 2-3 hours | 150% (time and a half) |
| After that | 200% (double time) |

Check your award — some have different thresholds.

## Night/Evening Work

Some awards have loadings for late shifts:
- Evening (e.g., after 7pm): 115-125%
- Night/overnight: 115-130%

## Public Holidays

Employees can refuse to work public holidays if the refusal is reasonable.

If they do work:
- Penalty rates apply (usually 250%+)
- May also get a substitute day off

See [[leave/public-holidays]] for the list of public holidays.
