# National Minimum Wage

The minimum any adult employee can be paid. Updated annually on 1 July.

## Current Rates (July 2025)

| Category | Hourly | Weekly (38 hrs) | Annual |
|----------|--------|-----------------|--------|
| Adult (21+) | $24.10 | $915.90 | $47,627 |
| Casual (incl. 25% loading) | $30.13 | N/A | N/A |

## Junior Rates

Employees under 21 can be paid a percentage:

| Age | % of Adult Rate | Hourly |
|-----|-----------------|--------|
| Under 16 | 36.8% | $8.87 |
| 16 | 47.3% | $11.40 |
| 17 | 57.8% | $13.93 |
| 18 | 68.3% | $16.46 |
| 19 | 82.5% | $19.88 |
| 20 | 97.7% | $23.55 |
| 21+ | 100% | $24.10 |

## Apprentice Rates

Apprentices have separate rates based on year of apprenticeship and age. Check the specific [[MOCs/awards|award]] for your industry.

General pattern:
- Year 1: ~55% of tradesperson rate
- Year 2: ~65%
- Year 3: ~80%
- Year 4: ~95%

## Award Rates vs Minimum Wage

Most employees are covered by an [[MOCs/awards|award]], which sets HIGHER rates than the minimum.

The national minimum wage only applies to:
- Award-free employees
- Where the award rate is somehow lower (rare)

## When It Changes

- Fair Work Commission reviews annually
- New rates apply from first full pay period on/after 1 July
- Announced in May/June
