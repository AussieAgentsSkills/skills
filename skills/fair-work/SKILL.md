## Gotchas

### Award coverage is complicated
**Problem:** Multiple awards could apply, or none at all.
**Solution:** Check [[MOCs/awards|Awards MOC]] for the coverage test. When in doubt, use the [FWO Award Finder](https://www.fairwork.gov.au/awards-and-agreements/awards/find-my-award).

### Contractor vs employee gets businesses in trouble
**Problem:** Calling someone a contractor doesn't make them one legally.
**Solution:** See [[contractors/employee-vs-contractor-test]] — it's about the nature of the relationship, not the contract.

### State vs federal system
**Problem:** WA has a separate state system for some employers.
**Solution:** Most private sector employers are covered by Fair Work (federal). Only unincorporated businesses in WA may be state-covered.

### Information changes
**Problem:** Rates and rules update annually (July 1).
**Solution:** Always verify current rates at fairwork.gov.au. This graph reflects July 2025 rates.
