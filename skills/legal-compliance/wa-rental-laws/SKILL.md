# WA Rental Laws

You are an expert in WA residential tenancy law under the Residential Tenancies Act 1987.

## Bond

### Amount
- Maximum 4 weeks rent
- Pet bond: Additional 1 week (if pets allowed)

### Lodgement
- Lodge with Bond Administrator within 14 days
- Tenant receives receipt

### Return
- Apply via Bond Administrator
- Landlord has 14 days to dispute
- DMIRS mediates disputes

## Rent Increases

### Frequency
- Fixed term: Only at end of lease or if lease allows
- Periodic: Maximum once per 6 months

### Notice
- 60 days written notice
- Can apply to Magistrates Court if excessive

## Notice Periods

### Tenant Ending Lease
| Situation | Notice |
|-----------|--------|
| End of fixed term | 21 days |
| Periodic lease | 21 days |
| Breaking fixed term | 21 days + compensation |

### Landlord Ending Lease
| Reason | Notice |
|--------|--------|
| No grounds (end of fixed) | 30 days |
| No grounds (periodic) | 60 days |
| Sale of property | 30 days |
| Breach | 14 days |
| Non-payment | 7 days |

## Repairs

### Urgent Repairs (24 hours)
- Burst water service
- Blocked toilet (only toilet)
- Gas leak
- Dangerous electrical fault
- Flooding
- Storm damage
- Failure of essential services

### Non-Urgent Repairs
- Request in writing
- Landlord has reasonable time (14-28 days)
- Can apply to Magistrates Court if not fixed

## Inspections

### Routine
- Maximum 4 per year
- 7-14 days written notice
- Between 8am and 6pm
- Tenant can be present

### Entry
- Emergency only without notice
- Otherwise need consent or notice

## Pets

- Landlord cannot unreasonably refuse
- Can impose conditions
- Pet bond up to 1 week rent
- Must repair any pet damage

## Breaking a Lease

Compensation may include:
- Lost rent until re-let
- Advertising costs
- Re-letting fee
- Maximum: rent for remainder of term

## Minimum Standards

All rentals must have:
- Functioning bathroom and toilet
- Hot and cold water
- Working stove/oven
- Adequate ventilation
- Secure locks on doors and windows

## Water Charges

Tenant pays usage if:
- Property is water efficient
- Separately metered
- Not in strata with shared meter

## Disputes

**Magistrates Court WA** handles:
- Bond disputes
- Repairs
- Compensation
- Termination

Application fee: ~$75-150
