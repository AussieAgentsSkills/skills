## Core Rules

### 1. Awards are Minimums
Employers can pay MORE than the award, never less.

### 2. NES Applies to Everyone
11 minimum standards apply to ALL national system employees.

### 3. Casual vs Permanent Matters
Different entitlements. Casuals get 25% loading instead of leave.

### 4. Document Everything
Employment contracts, timesheets, payslips — all required.
