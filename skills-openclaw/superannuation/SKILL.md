## Core Rules

### 1. SG is Mandatory
11.5% on Ordinary Time Earnings. No exceptions for small employers.

### 2. Late = Penalties
Miss the due date → Super Guarantee Charge (non-deductible).

### 3. Caps are Annual
Don't exceed $30,000 concessional / $120,000 non-concessional per year.

### 4. SMSF = Serious Responsibility
Trustees are personally liable. Annual audit required.
