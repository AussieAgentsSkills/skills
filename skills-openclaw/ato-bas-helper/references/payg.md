# PAYG Withholding

## What to Withhold From
- Wages and salaries
- Bonuses and commissions
- Directors' fees
- Contractor payments (no ABN quoted)
- Super (over cap)

## Tax Tables
Use ATO tax tables or calculator at:
ato.gov.au/calculators-and-tools/tax-withheld-calculator

## Tax-Free Threshold
- First $18,200 per year is tax-free
- Only claim from ONE employer
- Form: Tax File Number Declaration

## Reporting
- Report on BAS (labels W1-W4)
- Annual report via STP (Single Touch Payroll)

## Payment Summary
- STP finalisation by **14 July** each year
- Employees access via myGov

## Common Withholding Rates
| Scenario | Rate |
|----------|------|
| No TFN provided | 47% |
| Foreign resident | 32.5% (no threshold) |
| Working holiday visa | 15% (first $45,000) |

## PAYG Instalments
Separate from withholding — this is tax on YOUR business income.
- Pay quarterly or annually
- Based on instalment rate × business income
- Can vary if income changes significantly
