# BAS Due Dates

## Quarterly Lodgement
| Quarter | Period | Due Date |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Q1 | 1 Jul - 30 Sep | **28 October** |
| Q2 | 1 Oct - 31 Dec | **28 February** |
| Q3 | 1 Jan - 31 Mar | **28 April** |
| Q4 | 1 Apr - 30 Jun | **28 July** |

## Monthly Lodgement
Due: **21st of following month**

| Month | Due Date |
|-------|----------|
| July | 21 August |
| August | 21 September |
| ... | ... |

## Annual GST
Due: **28 February** (following the financial year)

## Late Lodgement Penalties
| Time Late | Penalty (small entity) |
|-----------|----------------------|
| 1-28 days | $313 |
| 29-56 days | $626 |
| 57-84 days | $939 |
| 85+ days | $1,252 |

*Note: Penalties can be remitted if you have a good compliance history.*

## Superannuation Due Dates
SG contributions due: **28th day after quarter end**
- Q1 (Jul-Sep): 28 October
- Q2 (Oct-Dec): 28 January
- Q3 (Jan-Mar): 28 April
- Q4 (Apr-Jun): 28 July
