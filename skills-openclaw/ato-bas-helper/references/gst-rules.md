# GST Rules

## Standard Rate
- **10%** on taxable supplies

## GST-Free Supplies (0%)
- Most basic food (not restaurant meals)
- Health services (Medicare eligible)
- Education courses
- Childcare
- Exports
- Precious metals (gold, silver, platinum)

## Input-Taxed Supplies (no GST, no credits)
- Financial services (loans, bank fees)
- Residential rent
- Sale of residential premises (not new)

## No GST
- Wages and salaries
- Government charges (council rates, stamp duty)
- Donations
- Private sales between individuals

## Registration Thresholds
| Business Type | Threshold |
|---------------|-----------|
| General | $75,000/year |
| Non-profit | $150,000/year |
| Taxi/rideshare | $1 (must register) |

## Tax Invoice Requirements
For purchases over $82.50 (inc GST):
- Supplier's ABN
- Date issued
- Brief description
- GST amount or statement "Total price includes GST"
- Supplier's identity/business name
